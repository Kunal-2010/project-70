Campus life

KENDRIYA VIDYALAYA SCHOOL-CAMPUS

Kendriya Vidyalaya School is a Central Board of Secondary Education school located in Nava Jail,Durg , India.It also has another branch in all over India. The pre-primary and early years learning programme at CPS is a system where the tenets of Montessori education are scientifically combined with tried and tested teaching methods, focussing on language development and logical thinking, for children to start their first steps into the education system.

ART CLASSROOM

To kindle a conducive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide, Kendriya Vidyalaya School has spared no effort or investment to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them to pursue their education with ease and comfort.

Smart Class – Interactive Digital classroom:

Kendriya Vidyalaya School was one of the first schools in India to have smart boards in all the classrooms and also a 5.1 theatre system in all classrooms.

Sports & games

Students are segregated into four houses namely Ashoka House, Shivaji House, Raman House and Tagore House. Intra-mural games and sports are conducted to promote healthy competitions among the four houses where the adrenaline is gushed up as they take part in all events energetically.
